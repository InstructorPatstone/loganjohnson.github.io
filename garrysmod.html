Combine HUD Bootup Plugin

This Helix framework plugin delivers an immersive boot sequence for Combine players, complete with audio stingers, interference effects, and staged HUD activation.

  • Triggers automatically on first character load or after connecting to a server.
  • Repeats on each fresh session or rejoin to reinforce Combine identity.
  • Includes bespoke audio cues and visual interference synced to the boot timeline.
  • HUD elements intentionally stay disabled until the sequence completes for dramatic reveal.

Place your .mp4 inside assets/videos/ (e.g., assets/videos/combine-bootup.mp4) so it plays here.
