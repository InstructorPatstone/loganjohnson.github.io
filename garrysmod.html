Combine HUD Bootup

The Combine HUD Bootup Plugin plays an immersive startup sequence when a Combine character loads in, setting the tone with interference, vocal confirmations, and staged UI reveals. It runs on first spawn, after reconnects, and any time a session is freshly initialized to keep immersion consistent.

Session-aware triggering Custom audio suite HUD gating logic Project Warfront support

Player Experience Goals

  • Create anticipation by delaying tactical HUD overlays until the "boot" completes.
  • Use Combine-styled voice comms and tones so players instantly know their faction context.
  • Simple client-side command that can toggle the boot-sequence for those who don't want to see it.
  • Ensure the sequence replays on reconnects without manual intervention.

Boot Sequence Flow

  1. 01 · Handshake Authentication ping fires on first spawn or reconnect and locks HUD widgets.
  2. 02 · Audio + Visual FX Layered static, servo chirps, and vignette pulses guide the player's attention.
  3. 03 · System Verification Status text iterates through suit subsystems while gameplay input remains available.
  4. 04 · HUD Online UI modules render once the timeline completes, accompanied by a confirmation sting.

  • Lua handlers pace the sequence without blocking Helix hooks.

  • HUD elements register callbacks so widgets remain hidden until the final cue.
  • Audio bus isolation prevents other ambience from competing with the bootup sting.

Resistance Teleporter Construction Entity

Players can deploy the entity, designate any safe coordinate on the map, and watch as multiple allies, props, or even vehicles phase through a particle tunnel to the destination.

  • Fully animated, interactable map view lets players select any in-bounds coordinate.
  • Supports batching multiple players, physics entities, props, and vehicles through the same jump.
  • Destination zone displays a high-energy orb so defenders know an arrival is imminent.
  • Unique particle layers and light surges fire throughout the teleportation window.
  • Terrain validation cancels requests that would place users outside navigable space.
  • Designed for Project Warfront but easily configurable to work independently.

Vehicle Deployment Depot

A Helix-friendly depot entity that streamlines how factions requisition vehicles. Interacting with the structure displays a menu of assets, handles queuing, and spawns the chosen vehicle directly onto an aligned pad.

  • Context-sensitive menu only appears when players interact with the depot shell, keeping clutter off the HUD.
  • Every vehicle entry lists cost, slot count, and cooldown so players understand the queue before committing.
  • Adjustable queue length and timer tuning live in a config file, making balance passes painless.
  • Selected vehicles materialize on the spawn pad with faction skins applied and physics primed.
  • Safety checks block spawns if the pad is occupied, preventing accidental pileups.

Mobile Artillery Towing System

This Helix plugin upgrades artillery logistics by letting operators hitch gun platforms to approved vehicles, tow them across the map, then redeploy faster than dragging entities by hand.

  • Artillery pieces display when a compatible hitch vehicle is nearby, reducing guesswork during setup.
  • Attachment flow locks the cannon to the towing chassis, syncing physics constraints for stable transit.
  • On arrival, crews can detach and instantly resume manual firing without reconfiguration.
  • Server-side validation ensures only authorized classes initiate towing to prevent griefing.

Helix ↔ LVS Hunter-Chopper Overhaul

Built a custom integration layer between the Helix framework and LVS so players can field the Hunter-Chopper with fully reactive Combine UI, cockpit-ready animations, and an audio suite crafted from scratch.

  • Overhauled crosshair HUD with Combine styling, dynamic reticles, and charge indicators that pulse with player input.
  • Engine startup, rotor wash, flight loops, and main gun charge all use audio to match the faction aesthetic.
  • Extended LVS to respect Helix animation states so the pilot playermodel sits correctly without snapping or T-posing.
  • Reactive elements highlight throttle, yaw, and weapon state, giving real-time feedback in the HUD.

Improved Deployable Manhack

A tactical Helix inventory item that spawns a manhack drone. The drone shadows its owner, intercepts nearby hostiles, and intelligently returns to formation once the threat is cleared, all while respecting faction relationships.

  • Deploy action spawns the manhack at the player’s position and begins a throttled follow loop for performance.
  • When a valid enemy enters range, the drone breaks formation, attacks, then reacquires its owner after the kill.
  • Self-destruct safeguards trigger on player death or character swap to prevent orphaned NPCs.
  • No-collide filters keep the manhack from damaging its owner or same-faction allies in close quarters.
  • Advanced Helix relationship tables ensure it exclusively targets opposing players/NPCs.
