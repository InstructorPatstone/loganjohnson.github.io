Unreal Engine 5

An atmosphericmaze prototype showcasing cinematic environments, and effects to build sci-fi level ambience.

Maze Level Walkthrough

My first UE5 environment designed for a simple maze game where the player must run through and destroy coins while running from enemies.

Level Design Focus

  • Atmospheric dark environment giving a erie but futuristic feel.
  • Entities and models added to certain hallways to enhance immersion and grab the player attention and draw to move that direction.

Particles & Atmosphere

  • Fog ribbons and dust motes drift through cracked ceiling panels to sell abandoned infrastructure.
  • Localized ember bursts trigger near malfunctioning panels, synced to reactive audio cues.
  • Performance budget stays stable with LOD-driven particle culling and shared sprite sheets.
